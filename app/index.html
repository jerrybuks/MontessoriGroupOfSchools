ABOUT US


What we do…

MontessoriGroupOfSchools is an oragnized body of schoools owned by the same people across diferrent parts of Nigeria. MontessoriGroupOfSchools applies the Montessori method of education developed by Maria Montessori, in raising kids. Our Vision is to raise children that are well shaped and developed to bring about a societal transformation in the future. We intend to do this by setting up diffferent branches across the country, so as to reach a reasonable number of kids.

We believe strongly that Children are leaders of tommorow, but could become something entirerly different, if they are not giving the proper training they deserve.We have come to build the bridge from childhood to leadership. We hope to achieve this by designing a well structured sytem that equip kids with the right foundation to become tommorow leaders.

We are currently scattered across different parts of Nigeria and intend to grow across Africa and beyond in coming years. Our locations are currently in the following states: Abuja, Enugu, Imo, Lagos, and Ondo.
